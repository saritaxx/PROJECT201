What is the significance of acquiring both soft and hard skills before embarking on a career?

Posted by Sara Acevedo on April 17, 2023

Personal Experience

As someone who has gained experience in the workforce, I can attest to the significance of acquiring both soft and hard skills. Throughout my career, I have realized that having strong communication skills, being able to work collaboratively with others, and possessing problem-solving abilities have been critical to building relationships and achieving success in my roles

Additionally, being proficient in specific technical skills, such as QuickBooks, HTML, Excel, Vista, and bookkeeping, has helped me demonstrate my expertise in a particular area of work and make valuable contributions to my team. I have found that having a combination of soft and hard skills has made me more attractive to potential employers and opened up more job opportunities for me.

Every skill you acquire doubles your odds of success.

I believe that continually developing and honing these skills is essential to stay competitive and excel in one's career.
